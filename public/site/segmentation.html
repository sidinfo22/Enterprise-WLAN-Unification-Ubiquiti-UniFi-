Segmentation Model

VLANs keep Summit Care traffic organized and secure. Guest users should never be able to reach the domain controller, Cloud Key, management interfaces, printers, clinical systems, or internal servers.

NetworkExample VLAN/SubnetPurpose
ManagementVLAN 10 / 10.10.10.0/24Cloud Key, gateway, switch, and AP administration.
Staff Wi-FiVLAN 20 / 10.20.20.0/24Domain-authenticated staff devices using RADIUS/certificates.
Clinical DevicesVLAN 30 / 10.30.30.0/24Approved clinic equipment and internal systems.
Guest Wi-FiVLAN 40 / 10.40.40.0/24Internet-only visitor access with captive portal.
VPN UsersVPN client poolRemote access to approved internal networks only.
